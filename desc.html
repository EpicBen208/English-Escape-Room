Locked in a Bathroom Escape room



In this escape room we have added questions that respond to the literacy terms as well as it responds to some hidden meaning within the poem. Our clues work by reading the question then following up with the full stanza to figure out its meaning or what literary device is used with it. Many of the questions refer to the literacy terms studied in class or mentioned in poems we have already done. In conclusion the only way to escape id figure out the inner meaning as the literacy term used.
